RAVENCLAW

Founder : Rowena Ravenclaw

Common Room : Ravenclaw Tower

Ghost : Grey Lady

Traits : Wise

Animal : Eagle

Description :

Ravenclaw corresponded to the element of air. The Ravenclaw common room and dormitories were located in a tower on the west side of the castle. Ravenclaw students must answer a riddle as opposed to giving a password to enter their dormitories. This riddle, however, could be answered by non-Ravenclaws. Famous Ravenclaws included Luna Lovegood, Gilderoy Lockhart, Ignatia Wildsmith, and Garrick Ollivander.
