SLYTHERIN

Founder : Salazar Slytherin

Common Room : Slytherin Dungeon

Ghost : Bloody Baron

Traits : Cunning

Animal : Snake

Description :

Slytherin corresponded roughly to the element of water. The Slytherin dormitories and common room were reached through a bare stone wall in the Dungeons. The Slytherin common room lay beneath the Black Lake. It was a long, low underground room with rough stone walls and silver lamps hanging from the ceiling. Famous Slytherins included Merlin, Tom Riddle, Draco Malfoy, and Dolores Umbridge.
