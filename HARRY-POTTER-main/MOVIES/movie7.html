Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 is the first instalment of a two-part film based on Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows by J. K. Rowling.

David Yates, who directed the preceding two films, directed both parts, with Steve Kloves returning to script. The first part was released on November 19, 2010, with the second following in July 2011. Production of the two films occurred concurrently, and treated as if it were one film.
